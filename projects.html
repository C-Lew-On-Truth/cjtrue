Real Rap Fans

Whats this??

FOR REAL RAP FANS ONLY!

Real Rap Fans is my very first published web project hosted on GitHub! It is a desktop and labtop user friendly quiz which test any real rap on their overall knowledge with rap artist with respect to their lyrics and sound. Though it was challenging as a beginner, this project gave me a great foundation on front-end development via HTML, CSS, and JavaScript To play this game.

Just click on the spinning record to begin!

Thank you, good luck, and enjoy!

*NOT COMPATIBLE WITH MOBILE DEVICES AT THIS TIME*
